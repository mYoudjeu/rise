OUR MISSION

The vision of the RiseUpFoundation is to empower individuals and communities through training and supporting programs that foster social and economic inclusion , with a particular focus on those who are marginalized or facing significant barries to success.

OUR VISION

A society where Everyone has equal access to opportunities and where poverty and social exclusion are limited.

OUR VALUE

The RIseUP Foundation has a zero tolerance policy against abuse and exploitation of children and vulnerable persons. We hold firm to the conviction that good health and education at all levels are central to helping break the intergenerational cycles of poverty.
