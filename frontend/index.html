About Us

We Help People In Need

We believe in humanity

We inspire communities to strive for a better future

We believe in African Entrepreneurship and Emergence. So we built an infrastructure that will be a reliable and trusted provider of support. The RiseUp Foundation will be the leading champion of ONG in Africa. Our objective is to empower women and men across our continent, catalyzing economic growth, driving poverty eradication and ensuring job creation. We believe the private sector’s role is critical for Africa’s development and that the private sector must create both social and economic wealth.
